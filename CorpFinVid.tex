A share of IBM is trading at $200. In 6 months it will either go up by $50 or down by $50. In the
following 6 months it will again either go up by $50 or down by $50. During this period IBM will pay 
no dividends. The 6 month interest rate on Treasuries is 1% and will stay that way for the full year.
(a) Value a European put option on IBM with a strike price of $225. (9 marks) 
(b) Value an American put option on IBM with a strike price of $225. Comment on the additional 
option value an American option provides. Do all American options provide additional value? 
(9 marks)
