\frametitle{Computing the Z-score}

The normal distribution has the following paramters

\begin{itemize}
\item $\mu$ the mean of the normal distribution
\item $\sigma$ the standard deviation of the distribution
\end{itemize}


\[z = \frac{x - \mu}{ \sigma}\]

Suppose $ \mu$ =1000
$\sigma=400$

\[ X \sim N(1000,400) \]
